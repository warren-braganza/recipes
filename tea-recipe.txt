Tea Recipe

Author: Warren Braganza
Created On: 12/05/2016
Updated On: 12/05/2016

Ingredients:
Tea Powder
Sugar
Water
Milk

Instructions:
1. First add 3 cups of water to a stainless steel container.
2. Add 3 tsp tea powder and 1 tbsp sugar.
3. Bring the mixture to a boil.
4. When the mixture is at a boiling point, add milk.
5. Allow the tea to boil and switch of the stove.
6. Serve in a tea cup or glass, accompanied by bread and cheese.
